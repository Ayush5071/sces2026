Holy Place – Varanasi (Kashi)

  • Distance from Prayagraj: ~120 km
  • Nearest Airport: Lal Bahadur Shastri International Airport
  • Railway Station: Varanasi Junction
  • Major Attractions: Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Dashashwamedh Ghat, Ganga Aarti
  • Famous Foods: Kachori Sabzi, Malaiyo, Banarasi Paan

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: October – March
  • Local transport: E-rickshaws, taxis, autos
  • Carry light cotton clothes during summer
