Call for Papers

Following are the technical tracks and topics for the technical papers to be submitted in SCES-2026.

Power, Energy & Control

Control Systems, Instrumentation & Measurement, Dielectrics & Electrical Insulation, EHV AC & HVDC Transmission, Electric Vehicles, Energy Storage, Industrial Drives, Power Electronics, Smart Grid & Micro Grid Technologies, Robotics & Automation.

Electronics, Communication & Signal Processing

Antennas & Propagation, Communication Systems, Micro & Nano-electronics, Optical & RF Communication, Sensors, VLSI Design, Signal, Image & Video Processing, Wireless Technologies.

Computer Networks & Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Big Data Analytics, IoT, Wireless Networks, 5G & Cloud Computing.

Mechanical Systems & Mechatronics

Aerospace Engineering, CFD, Composite Materials, Finite Element Methods, Manufacturing Technology, MEMS, Smart Materials, Solid Mechanics.

Applied Sciences

Biomedical Engineering, Bioinformatics, Nano-technology, Geoscience & Remote Sensing, Nuclear Science, Engineering in Medicine & Biology, Statistical Methods.

Best Paper Awards

A Best Paper Award will be given in each technical track.
